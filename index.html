Welcome! I am Linxuan Li (李林轩), a Ph.D. student majoring in Geophysics in the Seismo Lab at California Institute of Technology. In 2023, I received my B.S. degree in Geophysics at Wuhan University. I am keen on everything associated with earthquakes. My research integrates observational data and theoretical models, encompassing both statistical and mechanical aspects. I hope to devote my life to exploring the phenomena associated with and the physical processes behind earthquakes so that we can better understand our planet, produce clean energy, and mitigate natural disasters.
