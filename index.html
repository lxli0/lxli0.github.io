Welcome! I am Linxuan Li (李林轩), a Ph.D. student majoring in Geophysics in the Seismo Lab at California Institute of Technology. In 2023, I received my B.S. degree in Geophysics at School of Geodesy and Geomatics, Wuhan University.

I am keen on everything associated with tectonics, especially earthquakes. My research focuses on both observational data and theoretical models. I use techniques from disciplines such as seismology, geodmechanics, geodesy, math, and physics. I hope to devote my life to exploring the phenomena associated with and the physical processes behind earthquakes so that we can better understand our planet, produce clean energy, and mitigate natural disasters.
